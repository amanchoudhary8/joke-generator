About Website

Developed by Sahil, Navneet and Aman

This is a Random Joke Generator that generates a new joke every time you click on the new joke button.You can also tweet the joke by clicking on the tweet button.The project was submitted to Syed Aamiruddin sir.
Some of the cool features of the project includes :

i.) Fetching of a new joke every time you click on "new joke" button.
ii.) Showering of emojis on clicking the “new joke” button.
iii.) Tweet button : the tweet button changes its shape on hover from hexagonal to hexagon with circular sides.
iv.) Logo also change its color on hover.
v.) Navigation bar fixed at the top of the web page and drop down buttons are also used.
vi.) Three different themes:Light, Dark and Solarize(which change color both in dark and light theme) in website.
vii.) Custom Scrollbar is used in website.
